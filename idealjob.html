Must possess the ability to communicate information security and risks to board members and specialists, become a strategic leader who has both the vision and ability to lead and motivate teams in the company to better themselves. Must hold security management certification or credential.

My respectful nature means that I am very approachable which allows me to keep calm and navigate through stressful situations while keeping a cool head.
